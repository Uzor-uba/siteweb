History

the saxophone was invented by Adolphe Sax in 1840 who created it as a hybrid instrument

combining the best features of the clarinet and the horn it was designed to be a more versatile instrument than its predecessors.

Saxophone Family

Learn about the different types of saxophones.

Types of Saxophones

  • Soprano Saxophone

    • The soprano saxophone is the smallest and highest-pitched member of the saxophone family.

  • Alto Saxophone

    • The alto saxophone is the most commonly used saxophone in jazz and classical music.

  • Tenor Saxophone

    • The tenor saxophone has a rich, warm tone and is popular in both jazz and popular music.

  • Baritone Saxophone

    • The baritone saxophone produces a deep, resonant sound and is often used in military bands and orchestras.
